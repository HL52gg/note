黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯可积
#

令\(f,g \in B([a,b])\), \(\pi = \{x_0=a,x_1,\cdots,x_n=b\} \in \Pi[a,b]\), \(\sigma = \{ s_j | s_j\in[x_{j-1},x_j],x_{j-1}\leq s_j \leq x_j \}\). 定义

$$ \Delta g_j = g(x_j) - g(x_{j-1}) $$

黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯和定义为

$$ S(f,g,\pi,\sigma) = \sum_{\pi}f(s_j)\Delta g_j $$

其中, 称\(f\)为integrand, 称\(g\)为integrator. 如果存在实数\(I\)对所有\(\epsilon > 0\), 存在\(\pi_o\in\Pi[a,b]\), 使得对\(\pi_o\)所有的加细\(\pi\)和与之对应的\(\sigma\)有

$$ | S(f,g,\pi,\sigma) - I |< \epsilon $$

成立, 我们称\(I\)为\(f\)相对于\(g\)在\([a,b]\)上的黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯积分, 记作\(\int_{a}^{b}f\,dg\) 或 \(RS(f,g,a,b)\). 当\(g(x) = x\)时, 就是黎曼积分.

性质
#

线性性质
#

若\(f_1,f_2\in RS([g,a,b])\), 则对任何常数\(c_1,c_2\),我们有 (Apostol, p142-143)

  1. \(c_1f_1 + c_2f_2 \in RS([g,a,b])\)
  2. \(RS([c_1f_1 + c_2f_2,g,a,b]) = c_1Rs([f_1,g,a,b]) + c_2Rs([f_2,g,a,b])\)

若\(f\in RS([g_1,a,b]), f\in RS([g_2,a,b])\), 我们有

  1. \(f\in RS([c_1g_1+c_2g_2,a,b])\)
  2. \(RS([f,c_1g_1+c_2g_2,a,b]) = c_1RS(f,g_1,a,b) + c_2RS(f,g_2,a,b) \)

若\(c\in(a,b)\), 我们有

  1. \(RS[f,g,a,b] = RS[f,g,a,c]+ RS[f,g,c,b]\)

如果\(a < b,\int_{a}^{b}f\,dg\)存在, 定义\(\int_{b}^{a}f\,dg = -\int_{a}^{b}f\,dg\), 定义\(\int_{a}^{a}f\,dg = 0\).

分部积分
#

如果\(f\in RS[g,a,b]\), 那么\(g\in RS[f,a,b]\)且 (Apostol, p144或 Basic Anslysis IV, p113-114)

$$ \int_{b}^{a}f\,dg = f(t)g(t)|_a^b -\int_{b}^{a}g\,df $$

变量替换
#

令\(f\in RS[g,a,b]\), 令\(q\)是\([c,d]\)上严格单调递增函数且\(q(c) = a,q(d)=b\). 对\(x\in[c,d]\), 定义复合函数\(\alpha(x) = f(q(x)), \beta(x) = g(q(x))\), 我们有 (Apostol, p145)

$$ \int_{q(c)}^{q(d)}f(t)\,dg(t) = \int_{c}^{d}f(q(x))\,dg(q(x)) = \int_{c}^{d}\alpha(x)\,d\beta(x) $$

退化为黎曼积分
#

令\(f\in RS[g,a,b]\), 如果\(g'\)在\([a,b]\)上连续, 我们有 (Apostol, p146)

$$ \int_{a}^{b}f(x)\,dg(x) = \int_{a}^{b}f(x)g'(x)\,dx $$

阶梯函数作为integrators
#

令\(g\in B([a,b])\), 如果其只有有限多个跳跃不连续点, 且在相邻的两个不连续点之间函数值为常数, 称\(g\)为阶梯函数. 令\(g\)为阶梯函数, 假设其只有一个跳跃不连续点\(c\), 我们有(Baisc Analysis IV, p115-120 或 Apostol, p147-149)

  1. 令\(f\in B([a,b])\), 则\(f\in C([a,b])\)暗含\(f\in RS([g,a,b])\)且
    • 若\(c\in(a,b)\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(c)[g(c^+) - g(c^-)]\)
    • 若\(c = a\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(a)[g(a^+) - g(a)]\)
    • 若\(c = b\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(b)[g(b) - g(b^-)]\)
  2. 令\(f\in B([a,b])\). 如果\(c\in(a,b)\), 则要求\(f(c^-) = f(c),g(c^+) = g(c)\), 我们有
    • 若\(c\in(a,b)\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(c)[g(c) - g(c^-)]\)
    • 若\(c = a\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(a)[g(a) - g(a)] = 0\)
    • 若\(c = b\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(b)[g(b) - g(b^-)]\)
  3. 令\(f\in B([a,b])\). 如果\(c\in(a,b)\), 则要求\(f(c^+) = f(c),g(c^-) = g(c)\), 我们有
    • 若\(c\in(a,b)\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(c)[g(c^+) - g(c)]\)
    • 若\(c = a\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(a)[g(a^+) - g(a)]\)
    • 若\(c = b\), 则\(RS(f,g,a,b) = f(b)[g(b) - g(b)] = 0\)

如果阶梯函数\(g\)有有限个跳跃不连续点\(\{a\leq c_0,c_1,\cdots,c_n\leq b\}\), 假设\(f\in B([a,b])\), 则如果\(f\)在\(c_j\)处连续 或 \(f\)在\(c_j\)处左连续, \(g\)在\(c_j\)处右连续 或 \(f\)在\(c_j\)处右连续, \(g\)在\(c_j\)处左连续, 那么\(f\in RS([f,g,a,b])\)且

$$ RS(f,g,a,b) = f(a)(g(a^+) - g(a)) + \sum_{j=0}^{n}f(c_j)(g(c_j^+) - g(c_j^-)) + f(b)(g(b)-g(b^-)) $$

利用此结论, 我们可以得到欧拉求和公式(Apostol, p149)

单调函数作为integrators
#

证明见(Basics Analysis IV, p121-123 或 Apostol, p150-153)

令\(f\in B([a,b]), 令g\in B([a,b])\)为单调递增函数, 令\(\pi = \{x_0,\cdots,x_p\}\)是\([a,b]\)的任意划分. 定义

$$ \begin{align*} M_j & = sup_{x\in[x_{j-1},x_j]}f(x) \\ m_j & = inf_{x\in[x_{j-1},x_j]}f(x) \end{align*} $$

上达布和和下达布和分别定义为

$$ \begin{align*} U(f,g,\pi) & = \sum_{\pi}M_j\Delta g_j \\ L(f,g,\pi) & = \sum_{\pi}m_j\Delta g_j \end{align*} $$

显然, \(L(f,g,\pi) \leq S(f,g,\pi,\sigma) \leq U(f,g,\pi)\)

  1. 对\(\pi \preceq \pi'\), 我们有\(L(f,g,\pi) \leq L(f,g,\pi')\), \(U(f,g,\pi) \geq U(f,g,\pi')\)
  2. \(\forall \pi_1,\pi_2\in\Pi[a,b]\), 有\(L(f,g,\pi_1) \leq U(f,g,\pi_2)\)
  3. 定义 $$ \begin{align*} L(f,g) & = \sup \{ L(f,g,\pi)|\pi\in\Pi[a,b] \} \\ U(f,g) & = \inf \{ U(f,g,\pi)|\pi\in\Pi[a,b] \} \end{align*} $$ 则\(L(f,g),U(f,g)\)均有限, 且\(L(f,g) \leq U(f,g)\), 且\(U(f,g), L(f,g)\)分别为上/下黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯积分
  4. 如果\(L(f,g) = U(f,g)\), 则称\(f\)为黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯-达布可积, 记作\(RSD(f,g,a,b)\).

黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯等价定理
#

类比黎曼的条件, 我们可以建立黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯积分和黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯-达布积分之间的联系.

令\(f,g\in B([a,b])\)且\(g\)单调递增, 如果对划分\(\pi_0\preceq \pi\)有

$$ U(f,g,\pi) - L(f,g,\pi) < \epsilon $$

我们称\(f\)相对于\(g\)满足黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯条件.

令\(f,g\in B([a,b])\)且\(g\)单调递增, 则下列条件等价 (Apostol, p154)

  1. \(f\in RS([g,a,b])\)
  2. \(f\)相对于\(g\)满足黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯条件
  3. \(f\)是黎曼-斯蒂尔杰斯-达布可积, 即\(L(f,g) = U(f,g)\), \(RS(f,g,a,b)=RSD(f,g,a,b)\)

性质(续)
#

假设\(f,f_1,f_2\in RS([g,a,b])\)且\(g\)有界单调递增, 则 (Basic Analysis, p125-127)

  1. \(|f|\in RS([a,b])\)
  2. \(|\int_a^bf(x)\,dg(x)| \leq \int_a^b|f|\,dg(x)\)
  3. \(f^+ = max\{f,0\} \in RS([g,a,b])\)
  4. \(f^- = max\{-f,0\} \in RS([g,a,b])\)
  5. \(\int_a^b f(x)\,dg(x) = \int_a^b[f^+(x)- f^-(x)]\,dg(x)\)
  6. \(\int_a^b |f(x)|\,dg(x) = \int_a^b[f^+(x)+ f^-(x)]\,dg(x)\)
  7. \(f^2 \in RS([g,a,b])\)
  8. \(f_1f_2 \in RS([g,a,b])\)
  9. 如果存在\(m\)对所有\(x\in[a,b]\)有\(0< m \leq f(x) \), 则\(1/f\in RS([a,b])\)
  10. 令\(m= \inf_x f(x), M=\sup_x f(x)\), 则 $$ m(g(b)-g(a)) \leq \int_{a}^{b}f(x)\,dg(x) \leq M(g(b) -g(a)) $$
  11. 若在\([a,b]\)上\(f_1\leq f_2\), 则有(Apostol, p155)
    • \(f \geq 0 \implies \int_a^bf(x)\,dg(x) \geq 0\)
    • \(f_1\leq f_2 \implies \int_a^bf_1(x)\,dg(x) \leq \int_a^bf_2(x)\,dg(x)\)
  12. 若\(c\in(a,b)\), 则\(f\in RS(g,a,c),f\in RS(g,c,b)\)且 (Apostol, p143) $$ \int_a^b f(x)\,dg(x) = \int_a^c f(x)\,dg(x) + \int_c^b f(x)\,dg(x) $$

令\(g\)在\([a,b]\)上有界且单调递增, 令\(f\in B([a,b])\). 令\(c\in (a,b)\), 定义

$$ \begin{align*} \overline{\int_{a}^{b}}f(x)\,dg(x) & = U(f,g) \\ \underline{\int_{a}^{b}}f(x)\,dg(x) & = L(f,g) \end{align*} $$

我们有 (Basic Analysis IV, p126)

$$ \begin{align*} \overline{\int_{a}^{b}}f(x)\,dg(x) & = \overline{\int_{a}^{c}}f(x)\,dg(x) + \overline{\int_{c}^{b}}f(x)\,dg(x)\\ \underline{\int_{a}^{b}}f(x)\,dg(x) & = \underline{\int_{a}^{c}}f(x)\,dg(x) + \underline{\int_{c}^{b}}f(x)\,dg(x) \end{align*} $$

注意, 若\(h\in B([a,b])\) (Apostol, p153)

$$ \begin{align*} \overline{\int_{a}^{b}}(f+h)\,dg & \leq \overline{\int_{a}^{c}}f\,dg + \overline{\int_{c}^{b}}h\,dg\\ \underline{\int_{a}^{b}}(f+h)\,dg & \leq \underline{\int_{a}^{c}}f\,dg + \underline{\int_{c}^{b}}h\,dg \end{align*} $$

有界变差函数作为integrators
#

关于有界变差函数的性质, 见文章黎曼积分

  1. 令\(g\in BV([a,b])\), \(f\in RS(g,a,b)\), 则\(f\in RS([V_g,a,b])\), \(f\in RS([V_g - g,a,b])\)(Basic Analysis IV, p127-131)
  2. 若\(a\leq c \leq b\), 则\(\int_a^b f(x)\,dg(x) = \int_a^c f(x)\,dg(x) + \int_c^b f(x)\,dg(x)\)

令\(g\in BV([a,b])\), \(f,f_1,f_2\in RS(g,a,b)\)则

  1. \(f^2\in RS([g,a,b])\)
  2. \(f_1f_2\in RS([g,a,b])\)
  3. 如果存在正常数\(m\)对所有\(x\in[a,b]\)有\(|f(x)|>m\), 则\(1/f \in RS([g,a,b])\)
